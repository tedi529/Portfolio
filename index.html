About Me

Data Analyst with an accounting and finance background.
Looking to join an innovative data team.

I'm a data enthusiast with a background in accounting and financial analysis. After spending some time in these more descriptive business fields, I decided that I wanted to work at the forefront of using data innovatively to solve problems and streamline processes in business. This led me to the Data Analytics and Visualization certificate program at the University of Central Florida, where I gained experience in Python, SQL, databases, data manipulation, and machine learning through an intensive 250 contact hour program.

Now, I want to put these and my previous analytical skills to use in a team of driven and innovative data specialists looking to advance their work through the insights of information. Data knows no bounds, but we decide how to gain its insights.

Projects


Chicago Crime - A Machine Learning Exploration

An exploration of crime trends in the city of Chicago and a study on the performance of 4 classification machine learning models to predict arrests for reported crimes in 2016-2019. Includes an interactive prediction page using the Multi Layer Perception Neural Network Model.

Covid-19 Communication

A visualization and graphical representation of twitter communication about COVID-19 by US state governors from February 1 to April 3, 2020. Includes interactive visualizations by week and state.

New York Waste Analysis

An examination of trends in population, total and per capita refuse, and recycling to determine the state of waste production in NYC for the years 1992-2019. Waste production exhibited an almost steady per capita decrease in the years analyzed.

Tableau Citibike Analysis

A visual comparison of trends in NYC Citibike utilization for the month of February in the years 2019 and 2020. While total trips taken increased year over year, average trip length and rider age remained constant.

Starbucks ETL

A demonstration of the ETL process through sourcing data, cleaning in Jupyter Notebook, loading into a Postgres Database, and serving query results to a web interface through a FLASK app.
